About

It wasn't a hard decision to become a web developer. My first line of code was in QBasic when I was 12, and I was considered the "computer repair kid next door" in my neighborhood. When I started front-end development, and my background in digital art made it seem easy and fun. By the time I picked up Ruby, there was no turning back.

I believe I write code with a deeper understanding than most people at my level. Beacuse all code is written by humans for humans, my years of academics in psychology provide me a special insight into my work. With this unique set of skills, I can be very helpful to society in solving some of the world's biggest problems.
